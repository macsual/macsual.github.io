UAV Mission Planner

The UAV Mission Planner or UMP is a project that was undertaken by a group of final year undergraduate students in the Department of Computing at The University of the West Indies at Mona, Jamaica for their Capstone group project course. The Capstone project is required for all students majoring in Computer Science and is an opportunity for students to apply some of the concepts that they have been exposed to while studying at university.

This Capstone project does mission planning for unmanned aerial vehicles by accepting various operational parameters and applying a variety of Mathematical concepts from areas such as Graph Theory and Geometry in order to make a best effort at deriving a flight path consisting of way points that will allow a drone to maximise its coverage of a given search space. For more detailed reading, the project’s final report is available both as a one-page HTML document as well as in PDF format.

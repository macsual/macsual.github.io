Use Of Google Services

The styling for most of the website is provided by Material Design Lite in order to provide a pleasant experience. A specific privacy policy for MDL could not be found, so it is assumed that Google’s Privacy Policy applies to its usage. The fonts used throughout the site are from Google Fonts which addresses users’ privacy in its FAQ.

Cookies and Analytics

Besides the cookies and analytics used by Google through the site’s use of their services mentioned in the previous section, this website does not make use of any cookies or any form of analytics.

Security

The entire website is served over HTTPS using a recent version of TLS and supports HTTP Strict Transport Security (HSTS). Secure versions of linked external resources are provided unless an external resource is not available over a secure channel (to the best of my knowledge).
